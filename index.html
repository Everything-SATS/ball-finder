Real-time colour filter, not AI guessing

🔍 Golf Ball Finder

Points your camera at the rough and paints anything that looks like a white golf ball in bright pink, so it pops out of the grass.

Your phone will ask for camera access — needed to scan for the ball.
How it works: every pixel from your camera is checked in real time — is it bright, and is it close to white (low colour saturation)? Pixels that match get painted pink; everything else stays as normal video. It's simple colour maths, not object recognition, so it works best on a plain white ball against green grass, rough or leaves. Use the sensitivity slider to tune it for shade, dew or a dirty ball.
