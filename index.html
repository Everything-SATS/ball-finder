🔍 Golf Ball Finder

Points your camera at the rough and paints anything that looks like your ball colour in bright pink, so it pops out of the grass. Works for white or optic yellow balls.

Your phone will ask for camera access — needed to scan for the ball.
How it works: every pixel from your camera is checked in real time against your chosen ball colour — bright and low-saturation for white, or the right hue and brightness for optic yellow — with green rejected explicitly so sunlit grass and dew glare don't light up. Pixels that match get painted pink; everything else stays as normal video. Use the sensitivity slider to tune it for shade, dew or a dirty ball. A metal-detector-style beep speeds up the more of the white outlined box in the middle of the screen matches — aim that box at the ground as you walk. It briefly holds onto a beep even after a quick glimpse, so a fast pass over the ball still gets caught. Mute it any time with the 🔊 button.
