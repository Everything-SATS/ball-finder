🔍 Golf Ball Finder

Your phone will ask for camera access — needed to scan for the ball.
How to use it
  1. Pick White or Yellow to match your ball
  2. Point your phone at the ground — the camera scans and lights up any ball in bright pink
  3. A metal-detector-style beep speeds up as you get closer to a match — mute it with 🔊
  4. Can't find it? Nudge the Sensitivity slider up
