🔍 Golf Ball Finder

Points your camera at the rough and paints anything that looks like your ball colour in bright pink, so it pops out of the grass. Works for white or optic yellow balls.

Your phone will ask for camera access — needed to scan for the ball.
How it works: every pixel from your camera is checked in real time against your chosen ball colour — bright and low-saturation for white, or the right hue and brightness for optic yellow. Pixels that match get painted pink; everything else stays as normal video. It's simple colour maths, not object recognition, so it works best against green grass, rough or leaves. Use the sensitivity slider to tune it for shade, dew or a dirty ball.
